Ampayon National High School STE Program

What sets us apart is our STE Program for Grades 7 to 10 — programs dedicated to fostering strong academic foundations, prioritizing personal growth and holistic development, and empowering high-performing students who are eager to go above and beyond.

Why Join the STE Program at Ampayon National High School?

Ampayon National High School provides young learners with the education, skills, values, and is committed to ensure that students will receive a strong academic foundation while also promoting personal growth. Not only that, but the school also offers Special Science, Technology, and Engineering (STE). This special program is specifically designed in order to give students an advance educational system, and the opportunity to enhance their knowledge, critical thinking, problem-solving, and the experience that allow them to prepare themselves for future challenges with confidence, resilience, and integrity. So what are you waiting for? Enroll now on Ampayon National Highschool, and join the STE Program!!

What Makes Us Unique?

Our STE Program is more than just academics. It’s about fostering creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills. Students gain hands-on experience through modern laboratories, collaborative projects, and innovative teaching methods.

Unleash Your Potential

Ampayon National High School equips students with the tools to excel academically and thrive within the Caraga region. Our STE Program has a proven track record of preparing students in Butuan City for local academic excellence and future opportunities.
