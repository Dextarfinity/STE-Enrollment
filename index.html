Benefits

Advancements in Math, Science, Technology and Engineering

Provides students with a strong foundation in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics, preparing them for future careers in high-demand fields and equipping them with valuable critical thinking and problem-solving skills.
Explore and Dive into Research

Fosters a lifelong love of learning by encouraging curiosity, developing critical thinking skills, and preparing students for success in higher education and their chosen careers.
Gain Strong Work Ethic

Cultivates essential skills like discipline, perseverance, and a commitment to excellence, fostering personal growth and setting the foundation for future success in any endeavor.
Enhance Interests and Abilities

Allows students to explore and enhance their interests and abilities, fostering personal growth, boosting self-confidence, and providing a sense of fulfillment that enriches their academic journey and prepares them for success beyond the classroom.
Boosts Confidence

Provides a supportive and encouraging environment that helps students boost their confidence, empowering them to embrace challenges, pursue their academic goals with determination, and achieve their full potential.

Requirements

Minimum Required Grade

Students must achieve a minimum of at least 85% grade of Science, Math and English throughout the first to third grading periods.
Photocopy of card

Student may present photocopy of their card for evaluation to STE school evaluators.
Moral Report

Students must present possessing a favorable moral report.
